Services Healthcare & Life Sciences Integration Services

Secure, interoperable, audit-ready systems for sensitive health and life sciences data.

FHIR API Development

The focus is not just creating endpoints, but modeling health data correctly so patient, lab, diagnostic, and clinical records can move between systems with clarity and control.

  • Patient & Observation APIs

Healthcare Data Pipelines

I build data pipelines that convert messy CSV, Excel, JSON, API, or internal system data into structured, validated, and usable healthcare formats. Each pipeline is designed with raw data retention, transformation rules, validation errors, audit visibility, and downstream integration in mind.

Lab & Diagnostic Data Integration
